# EduBoost AI – Requirements Specification

## 1. Objective
The objective of EduBoost AI is to enhance student learning productivity by using artificial intelligence to automate note creation, quiz generation, revision planning, and doubt resolution from study materials.

## 2. Problem Statement
Students face difficulties in managing large volumes of study material, revising efficiently, and getting instant clarification for doubts. Existing tools are either generic or not personalized for individual learning needs.

## 3. Solution Overview
EduBoost AI provides an AI-powered learning assistant that converts uploaded study material into concise notes, practice quizzes, and personalized study plans while offering an intelligent chatbot for doubt-solving.

## 4. Functional Requirements
- Upload study materials (PDF, text, notes)
- Automatically generate summarized notes
- Generate quizzes (MCQs and short answers)
- Provide AI-based doubt resolution using uploaded content
- Create personalized daily and weekly study plans
- Track student learning progress

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
- Simple and user-friendly interface
- Fast AI response time
- Scalable system for large number of students
- Secure storage of user data
- High availability and reliability

## 6. Target Users
- School students (Classes 9–12)
- College students
- Competitive exam aspirants

## 7. Assumptions
- Users have basic internet access
- Uploaded content is text-based
- AI models are accessible via cloud services

## 8. Success Metrics
- Reduction in study preparation time
- Improved student engagement
- Positive feedback from users
